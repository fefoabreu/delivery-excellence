# A-CLM Voiceover Script
**Target duration: 5 minutes · ~640 words · Audience: Client Executives**

---

## Slide 1 — Title
*[~20 seconds]*

What you're looking at is A-CLM — Agentic Contract Lifecycle Management.
It's the operating model we've built for AI-governed deal approvals —
and today I want to walk you through the problem it solves, the model behind it,
and the business value it puts on the table for you.

---

## Slide 2 — The Status Quo Has a Cost
*[~45 seconds]*

Let's start with where we are today.

Seventy thousand approval transactions per year — across five deal approval stages —
managed manually through application workflows and email notifications.

That process consumes 280,000 man-hours annually at an average of four hours per transaction.
Deal cycles run for weeks. And every week your deal sits in an approval queue
is a week your clients are not achieving their desired outcomes —
and a week of revenue your business has not yet recognised.

The investment in AI has been made. The capability is there.
The gap is between technology capability and process execution — and that gap is the opportunity.

---

## Slide 3 — The Opportunity
*[~40 seconds]*

Here's the headline.

If we move to the Target scenario — reducing 70,000 transactions to 55,000 —
we eliminate 15,000 transactions that should not exist in the first place.
At the same time, AI reduces the average deal cycle from 18 days to 5.

That's $2.7 million in annual cost avoidance. 72% faster deal cycles.
And a process that gets better with every deal that goes through it.

Gartner's data backs this up — firms that have solved the approval bottleneck with AI
are reporting 40% reductions in contract cycle time and 15.8% revenue increases.

---

## Slide 4 — The A-CLM Model
*[~35 seconds]*

The model is built on six strategic pillars.

Agentic AI that routes approvals autonomously. Playbook enforcement that checks every contract
before it reaches a human. Predictive deal scoring that means approvers see a brief, not a document.
And a foundation in the platforms you already use — Salesforce, Microsoft, Google, AWS.

This is not the frontier. BCG, McKinsey, and Accenture have already embedded this in their operations.
This is the competitive standard.

---

## Slide 5 — The Deal Approval Process
*[~30 seconds]*

The process runs across five sequential stages —
from Opportunity Approval through Deal Shaping, Technical and Business review,
Pricing and Investment sign-off, and final Deal Approval.

At every gate, the deal is AI-validated. Empowerment tables determine who signs.
Regional rules reflect your local regulatory and commercial context.
And the approval playbook governs how every stage is executed.

---

## Slide 6 — The Agent Ecosystem
*[~40 seconds]*

What makes A-CLM different is the closed loop.

On one side, a Pipeline Agent is continuously reading your opportunity pipeline
and sending forward signals into the cockpit — what deal types are coming, what tier of approval
they'll need, and where bottlenecks are likely to form.

On the other side, a Delivery Agent is feeding back live metrics on every deal
that was approved and entered delivery — health status, budget burn, milestone progress.

Approved deals don't disappear from the system. They inform it.
The cockpit sits at the centre, and every cycle makes the model smarter.

---

## Slide 7 — Business Value
*[~35 seconds]*

Two levers — and we separate them deliberately.

Lever one is simplification: eliminate transactions that should not exist.
Not automate them faster — eliminate them. That's the 70K to 55K move.

Lever two is acceleration: for the transactions that remain, AI cuts the cycle from 18 days to 5,
reduces hours per transaction by 63%, and drives rework rates from 23% down to 8%.

Both levers are tracked. Neither is assumed.

---

## Slide 8 — Business Value Testing
*[~30 seconds]*

Every improvement introduced to A-CLM goes through Business Value Testing.

BVT works like a regression test suite for the process —
each new feature is measured against the 70,000-transaction baseline.
Did it reduce transactions? Shorten cycles? Improve compliance? 

If it doesn't deliver real gain, it doesn't ship.
And automation of low-value processes is explicitly off the table.

---

## Slide 9 — Governance & Responsible AI
*[~25 seconds]*

A-CLM is not an autonomous system.

It is human-supervised and policy-governed — empowerment tables, enterprise authorisation policies,
regional business rules, the approval playbook, legal compliance, and responsible AI
are the six tenets that govern every decision the model makes.

AI accelerates and informs. Humans retain authority at every gate.

---

## Slide 10 — Close
*[~20 seconds]*

This is your deal approval process — accelerated, governed, and closed-loop with delivery.

70,000 transactions. Weeks compressed to days. $2.7 million in cost avoidance at the Target scenario.
And a feedback loop that makes every approved deal smarter than the last.

The question isn't whether to make this move. It's how fast.
